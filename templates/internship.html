Internships/Programs

The overall goal of education is to adequately prepare students to achieve success in their future careers. While academic rigour and acquisition of important life skills can be considered crucial aspects of the student’s career journey, more and more importance is being placed on the value of experiential and applied to learn.

With the ever-growing changes in our economy, it has become imperative for the students to be a part of that growth cycle and constantly upskill their talents and experiences to stay ahead in the competitive job market. This is where internships, summer programs and job shadowing opportunities play a crucial role.
